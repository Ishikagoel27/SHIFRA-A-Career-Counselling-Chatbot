About Shifra

Shifra is a smart and friendly AI-based career counselor designed to help students explore and choose the right career path based on their interests, skills, and strengths. Whether you're confused about your future or just curious about where you fit best, Shifra is here to guide you.

It interacts like a real counselor, asking meaningful questions, analyzing your responses, and offering personalized career suggestions using machine learning. The goal is to make career discovery fun, stress-free, and accessible to everyone.
